Features

Everything you need to manage a remote team

Built by remote team managers, for remote team managers.

Automatic Time Tracking

The desktop app tracks time silently in the background. No manual timers, no forgotten start/stops. Just accurate data.

Smart Screenshots

Random screenshots at configurable intervals prove your team is on-task, without being invasive or micromanaging.

Activity Monitoring

Mouse clicks and keystrokes are counted per minute to calculate an activity percentage — no keylogging, ever.

App & URL Tracking

See which applications and websites your team uses during work sessions. Identify distractions or tools to invest in.

Payroll & Invoicing

Track pay rates, generate invoices for clients, and record expenses — all in one place. Simplify your finances.

Team Management

Invite members by email, assign them to projects and teams, set hourly rates, and control tracking permissions per person.

How it works

Up and running in 5 minutes

1

Sign up & create your team

Create your admin account, set up your organization, and add your projects with hourly rates and budgets.

2

Invite your team members

Send email invitations to your team. They set their own password and are linked to your workspace automatically.

3

Members download the desktop app

Your team downloads the tiny Trackora app for Windows or Mac. It runs silently in the background, no setup needed.

4

Track & manage from your dashboard

Watch your team's activity in real time. Review screenshots, timesheets, app usage, and export reports anytime.
